   tasks. This is an oversight. The task is routine but the box cutter risk
   is real and should have been addressed.

   removal tasks since his first week on site. No one identified the PPE gap
   during that period.

CONTRIBUTING FACTORS:
- The tool store does stock cut-resistant gloves (Level B, ANSI/ISEA 105)
  but they are not labelled for specific tasks — workers select gloves
  ad hoc, which means they may not know which glove is appropriate for which task.
- Sam had not been given a task-specific induction for strapping removal.
  The SWMS for "General Labouring" (SWMS-GL-003) does not specifically
  cover strapping / packaging removal.

   all cutting and strapping removal tasks — Jason / 21 Feb 2024

   with PPE requirements — Jason / 21 Feb 2024
