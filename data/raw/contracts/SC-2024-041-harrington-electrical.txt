SCHEDULE A — SCOPE OF WORKS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Harrington Electrical Services Pty Ltd ("Subcontractor") shall provide all
labour, materials, plant, and supervision necessary to carry out the following
electrical works at the Project site ("Works"):

A1. Primary Switchboard and Distribution
  - Supply, install, and commission one (1) main switchboard (MSB-01)
    rated 800A, 415V three-phase, with integral metering
  - Supply, install, and commission six (6) distribution boards (DB-01
    through DB-06) as per drawings E-101 through E-106 (Rev C)
  - All cable trays, conduits, and containment systems between MSB-01
    and distribution boards

A2. Power Outlets and Small Power
  - Install 320 x double GPO (10A) throughout Zones 1–4
  - Install 48 x three-phase outlets (32A) in Zones 2 and 3 (warehouse)
  - Install 12 x EV charging outlets (32A, Type 2) in car park Zone 5

A3. Lighting
  - Supply and install LED lighting to all internal areas as per
    lighting schedule (Document EL-Schedule-Rev2)
  - Emergency and exit lighting throughout — minimum 90-minute duration
  - External façade and car park lighting

A4. Fire Indicator Panel Interface
  - Interface with fire contractor's FIP (Contractor: Axiom Fire Systems)
  - Supply all cable between FIP and electrical equipment requiring
    monitored shutdown on fire alarm

A5. Testing and Commissioning
  - Test and tag all electrical installations
  - Thermographic inspection of all switchboards and distribution boards
  - Submit test results on Form TC-E-001

Clause 1 — Governing Law
  This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Victoria,
  Australia.

Clause 2 — Variations
  No variation to the scope of works will be recognised unless
  authorised in writing by BuildCore's Project Manager prior to the
  variation work being carried out. Verbal authorisations will not
  be accepted. Variation claims must be submitted on Form VO-001.

Clause 3 — Subcontracting
  The Subcontractor must not further subcontract any part of the Works
  without prior written approval from BuildCore.

Clause 4 — Termination for Convenience
  BuildCore may terminate this Agreement for convenience by giving
  14 days written notice. The Subcontractor will be entitled to payment
  for work satisfactorily completed to the date of termination plus
  reasonable demobilisation costs. No further compensation will be payable.

Clause 5 — Liquidated Damages
  If the Works are not practically complete by the date specified in
  this Agreement due to causes attributable to the Subcontractor,
  liquidated damages of $2,500 per calendar day will apply.

Clause 6 — Dispute Resolution
  Disputes must first be referred to the respective parties' project
  managers for resolution. If unresolved within 10 business days,
  disputes will be referred to mediation before any legal proceedings.
