SCHEDULE A — SCOPE OF WORKS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Apex Plumbing & Drainage Pty Ltd ("Subcontractor") shall provide all
labour, materials, plant, and supervision to complete the following
plumbing and drainage works:

A1. Stormwater Drainage
  - Install 280 linear metres of PVC stormwater drainage, 150mm–300mm
    diameter, as per drawing P-201 (Rev B)
  - Supply and install 8 x pit and grate assemblies
  - Connection to existing council stormwater network at pit SP-01

A2. Sanitary Drainage
  - Install internal sanitary drainage to amenities block (Zones 6 & 7)
  - Install all floor wastes, floor drains, and cleanouts
  - All drainage to comply with AS/NZS 3500.2

A3. Hot and Cold Water Services
  - Supply and install hot and cold water reticulation throughout
    amenities block and lunch room
  - Instantaneous gas hot water system (Rinnai or approved equivalent)
  - All pipework to comply with AS/NZS 3500.1

A4. Fire Hydrant and Hose Reel System
  - Extension of existing fire hydrant ring main — 40 linear metres
    of Class D galvanised steel pipe (65mm)
  - Installation of 2 x fire hose reel cabinets (Zones 2 and 3)
  - Hydraulic test of completed system at 1800 kPa for 60 minutes

A5. Backflow Prevention
  - Install testable double check valve assembly at water meter
  - Register with local council and arrange initial test
